Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Introduction

HealthCare Info (the "Application") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy describes how information is handled within the Application.

Information We Access

The Application reads the following health data from Apple HealthKit with your permission:

  • Body weight
  • Blood pressure (systolic and diastolic)
  • Heart rate
  • Step count
  • Other health data stored in HealthKit

This data is read directly from HealthKit on your device. The Application does not independently collect or store this data on its own servers.

Use of Health Data

Health data accessed from HealthKit is used solely for the following purposes within the Application:

  • Displaying charts and data lists within the app
  • Exporting data in CSV format (initiated by the user)

Health data is never transmitted to external servers and is never shared with third parties, including for advertising purposes.

Advertising

The Application uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and process the following information to provide advertising:

  • Device information
  • Advertising ID (IDFA)
  • Approximate location
  • App usage data

Information collected by AdMob may be used for personalized advertising. Health data accessed by this Application is never used for advertising purposes.

Data Storage

All data stored by the Application (such as notification settings and fasting timer settings) is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to external servers. Data collected by AdMob may be stored on Google's servers.

Third-Party Services

The Application uses Google AdMob for advertising. For more information on how AdMob handles data, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy.

Opt-out of Personalized Advertising

You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings. You can also change your ad privacy preferences from the "Measurement Settings" screen within the Application. For more information, please visit Google's Ad Settings page.

Children's Privacy

The Application is not directed to anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our Contact Page.
