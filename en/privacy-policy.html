Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Introduction

This Privacy Policy applies to the following mobazo apps:

  • Makidenlite (Scroll Paper Calculator)
  • FastingManager
  • Rain & Wind Weather Map

Each app respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy describes how information is handled within these applications.

Information We Collect

Our apps do not directly collect personal information. However, our apps use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and process the following information to provide advertising:

  • Device information
  • Advertising ID (IDFA)
  • Approximate location
  • App usage data

Health Data Access (FastingManager only)

FastingManager reads the following health data from Apple HealthKit with your permission:

  • Body weight
  • Blood pressure (systolic and diastolic)
  • Heart rate
  • Step count

This data is read directly from HealthKit on your device. It is never transmitted to external servers and is never shared with third parties, including for advertising purposes.

Use of Information

Information collected by AdMob may be used for personalized advertising and analytics purposes. Our apps do not access or use this information directly.

Data Storage

Our apps store user data locally on the device only and do not transmit it to external servers. Data collected by AdMob may be stored on Google's servers.

Third-Party Services

Our apps use Google AdMob for advertising. For more information on how AdMob handles data, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy.

Opt-out of Personalized Advertising

You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings. In FastingManager, you can also change your ad privacy preferences from the "Measurement Settings" screen. For more information, please visit Google's Ad Settings page.

Children's Privacy

Our apps do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our Contact Page.
